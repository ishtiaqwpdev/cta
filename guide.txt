This guide explains how your learning and supervision website works in simple
steps. You do not need to know coding. Most day-to-day work is done from the
WordPress admin area (your website control panel).


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
PART 1 — BIG PICTURE: WHAT DOES THIS SYSTEM DO?
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Your website helps two types of people:

  A) LICENSED PROFESSIONALS (LMFT, LCSW, LPCC, LEP)
     - Browse and buy CE (Continuing Education) courses
     - Watch video lessons module by module
     - Pass a quiz and download a certificate
     - Use the CE Student Dashboard

  B) REGISTERED ASSOCIATES (AMFT, ASW, APCC)
     - Subscribe to supervision plans
     - Book group or individual supervision sessions
     - Upload BBS documents and track hours
     - Use the Supervision Dashboard

Everything is managed by the "CTA LMS" plugin inside WordPress. Payments go
through Stripe (credit card). When someone pays, they automatically get access.

PART 3 — WHAT IS A "SHORTCODE"? (SIMPLE EXPLANATION)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

A shortcode is a small piece of text you place on a WordPress page. It tells
the system what to show on that page — like a login form, course list, or
booking calendar.

Example:  [cta_login_form]

You normally do NOT need to change shortcodes. They were set up when the site
was built. If a page looks empty, check that the correct shortcode is on that
page in WordPress → Pages → Edit.

SHORTCODE CHEAT SHEET — WHICH CODE GOES ON WHICH PAGE
------------------------------------------------------

  Page purpose                    Shortcode to use
  ------------------------------  ------------------------------------------
  Login page                      [cta_login_form]
  All courses catalog             [cta_course_catalog]
  Home (featured courses only)    [cta_course_catalog limit="3"]
  Single course detail            [cta_single_course]
  Memberships & bundles           [cta_membership_pricing]
  Supervision booking             [cta_supervision_booking]
  CE student dashboard            [cta_student_dashboard]
  Supervision associate dashboard [cta_supervision_dashboard]
  Course video player             [cta_course_player]
  Course quiz                     [cta_quiz]
  Login / Dashboard button        [cta_auth_button]
  (optional header/footer)        [cta_header]  and  [cta_footer]

The full list is also inside WordPress:
  WordPress Admin → CTA LMS → Shortcodes

PART 4 — HOW TO LOG IN TO WORDPRESS (ADMIN)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

STEP 1: Open your browser and go to:
        https://cta.techosuppglobal.com/wp-admin/

STEP 2: Enter your WordPress username and password.

STEP 3: After login you will see the WordPress dashboard on the left side.

STEP 4: Click "CTA LMS" in the left menu. This is your main control area for

CTA LMS ADMIN MENU — WHAT EACH SECTION DOES
--------------------------------------------

  Dashboard   — Overview: enrollments, revenue, recent activity
  Courses     — Add, edit, and publish CE courses
  Users       — See registered students and associates
  Bookings    — Create supervision sessions for associates to book
  Settings    — Stripe payments, page links, certificates, support email
  Shortcodes  — Reference list of all shortcodes


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
PART 5 — HOW TO ADD A NEW CE COURSE (STEP BY STEP)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

STEP 1: WordPress Admin → CTA LMS → Courses

STEP 2: Click "Add New Course"

STEP 3: Fill in the course details:
        - Course Title          (name students will see)
        - Category              (e.g. Ethics, Clinical Skills)
        - CE Hours              (how many CE credits the course is worth)
        - Price                 (use 0 for a free course)
        - Description           (full course overview)
        - Learning Objectives   (bullet points — click "Add Objective")
        - Thumbnail URL         (course image link)
        - Preview Video         (Vimeo, YouTube, or upload from Media Library)
        - Status                — choose "Published" to show on the website
                                  choose "Draft" to hide while you work on it

STEP 4: Click "Save Course"

STEP 5: After saving, scroll down on the same page to add MODULES (lessons).


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
PART 6 — HOW TO ADD MODULES (VIDEO LESSONS) TO A COURSE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Modules are the individual lessons inside a course. Students complete them in
order (one after another).

STEP 1: Open the course: CTA LMS → Courses → click Edit on the course

STEP 2: Scroll to the "Course Modules" section

STEP 3: For each module, enter:
        - Module title
        - Description (short text under the title)
        - Video Source — Vimeo ID, YouTube link, WordPress Media, or MP4 link
        - Duration (minutes)
        - "Locked until previous complete" — leave checked so students must
          finish lesson 1 before lesson 2 opens

STEP 4: Click "Add Module"

STEP 5: Repeat for every lesson in the course

STEP 6: You can drag modules to change the order


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
PART 7 — HOW TO ADD A QUIZ TO A COURSE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Every CE course should have one final quiz. Students must pass it to get their
certificate.

STEP 1: On the same course edit page, scroll to "Course Quiz"

STEP 2: Enter a Quiz Title (e.g. "Final Assessment")

STEP 3: Click "+ Add Question" for each multiple-choice question:
        - Type the question
        - Add answer options (A, B, C, D)
        - Mark which answer is correct

STEP 4: Click "Create Quiz" or "Save Quiz"

IMPORTANT: Students can only take the quiz AFTER they complete ALL modules.


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
PART 8 — WHAT STUDENTS SEE: BUYING & TAKING A COURSE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

This is the journey for a LICENSED PROFESSIONAL:

STEP 1 — BROWSE
  Student visits the courses page or home page and sees available courses.

STEP 2 — VIEW COURSE
  Student clicks a course and sees the course detail page with price and
  module list.
  BEFORE buying: all modules show as LOCKED (lock icon). They cannot watch
  videos yet.

STEP 3 — CREATE ACCOUNT OR LOG IN
  Student goes to the Login page and either logs in or clicks Register.
  They must choose: "Licensed Professional (LMFT, LCSW, LPCC, LEP)"

STEP 4 — ENROLL / PAY
  On the course page they click "Enroll Now" (or similar).
  - Paid course → Stripe checkout (credit card)
  - Free course (price $0) → instant enrollment
  - Bundle purchase on Memberships page can enroll them in multiple courses

STEP 5 — CE DASHBOARD
  After login, licensed professionals go to the CE Student Dashboard.
  They ONLY see courses they have purchased or enrolled in.
  Courses they did not buy do NOT appear here.

STEP 6 — WATCH LESSONS
  Student clicks "Continue Learning" → opens the Course Player.
  They watch Module 1, mark it complete, then Module 2 unlocks, and so on.

STEP 7 — TAKE QUIZ
  After all modules are done, the final quiz unlocks.
  They must reach the passing score (set in the quiz).

STEP 8 — CERTIFICATE
  After passing the quiz, a certificate is available in the dashboard under
  "My Certificates". They can download it as a PDF.

PART 12 — SUPERVISION BOOKING PAGE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

URL: https://cta.techosuppglobal.com/supervision-booking/
Shortcode: [cta_supervision_booking]

This page has three main parts:

  1) SUPERVISION PLANS (pricing cards)
     - Group Supervision: monthly subscription (~$260/month)
       • 4 group sessions per month, 2 hours each
       • Max 8 associates per group (California BBS rule — automatic)
     - Individual 1-on-1: pay per session (~$120/session)
       • 60-minute private session

  2) HOW IT WORKS (4 steps shown on the page)
     Choose plan → Create account → Book sessions → Attend & track hours

  3) BOOKING CALENDAR
     Shows available session dates and times

CUSTOMER JOURNEY (ASSOCIATE):

STEP 1: Register as "Registered Associate (AMFT, ASW, APCC)"

STEP 2: Log in and visit Supervision Booking page

STEP 3: For GROUP supervision:
        Click Subscribe → pay monthly via Stripe → subscription becomes active

STEP 4: For INDIVIDUAL session:
        Pay per session when booking (no monthly subscription needed)

STEP 5: Scroll to booking calendar and click an available session to book

STEP 6: Confirmed sessions appear in their Supervision Dashboard

IF NOT LOGGED IN:
  Buttons say "Login to Book" and the calendar shows a login message.

PART 13 — HOW YOU (ADMIN) ADD SUPERVISION SESSIONS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Associates can only book sessions that YOU create first.

STEP 1: WordPress Admin → CTA LMS → Bookings

STEP 2: Make sure you are on the "Upcoming Sessions" tab

STEP 3: Click "Add New Session"

STEP 4: Fill in:
        - Date
        - Time
        - Type: Group or Individual

STEP 5: Save the session

STEP 6: The session now appears on the Supervision Booking page calendar
        for associates to book (if they have an active plan)

STEP 7: Use "Session History" tab to see past sessions and who attended

TIP: Create sessions several weeks in advance so associates always have
dates to choose from.

PART 15 — PAYMENTS (STRIPE) — WHAT YOU NEED TO KNOW
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

All online payments use Stripe (credit/debit cards).

SETTINGS LOCATION:
  WordPress Admin → CTA LMS → Settings → Stripe Configuration

  - Test mode  = for trying purchases without real money
  - Live mode  = real payments from customers

BEFORE GOING LIVE:
  1. Enter your Live Stripe keys in Settings
  2. Turn OFF "Skip payments" (testing mode) in Settings
  3. Do a test purchase with a real card for a small amount

WHAT STRIPE HANDLES AUTOMATICALLY:
  - Course enrollments after payment
  - Supervision subscriptions (monthly billing)
  - Bundle purchases
  - Failed payment retries and subscription locking

PART 19 — COMMON QUESTIONS (FAQ FOR CLIENT)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Q: I added a course but it does not show on the website.
A: Make sure Status is set to "Published", not "Draft".

Q: Student says modules are locked on the course page.
A: That is normal before they enroll. After payment, modules unlock in the
   Course Player (dashboard), not on the public course page preview list.

Q: Student bought a course but dashboard is empty.
A: Check they are logged into the correct email. Check CTA LMS → Users.
   Payment may have failed — check Stripe dashboard.

Q: Associate cannot book a session.
A: They need an active supervision subscription (group) or must pay for
   individual session. Also check that you created sessions in Bookings.

Q: How do I change prices on the Memberships page?
A: Contact your developer — bundle prices are managed in the system database.

Q: How do I add courses to the home page?
A: The home page uses [cta_course_catalog limit="3"] to show 3 featured
   courses. Publish courses in WordPress admin and they appear automatically.

Q: Can one person use both CE courses and supervision?
A: Yes, but they should register with the account type that matches their
   primary need. Associates get the Supervision Dashboard. Licensed
   professionals get the CE Dashboard. Hybrid plan gives both types of access.
